Research Interests

I am broadly interested in research topics related to programming languages, software engineering and operation systems, including program analysis, program verification, program synthesis, mobile computing, cloud computing and security. Currently my research mainly focuses on program analyses for large-scale programs. I have also worked on mobile-cloud computing and approximate computing. If you are interested in my work, please do not hesitate to contact me.
