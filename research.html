Research Interests

I work in the intersection of formal verification and machine learning. On one hand, I apply formal verification and programming language techniques to improve interpretability, safety, fairness, and generalizability of machine learning models. On the other hand, I improve program analysis and enable its new applications by incorporating probabilistic reasoning and data-driven approaches.
